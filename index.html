Mother's Day 2015 A Story About Love

Mother & Baby Unite


Once upon a time...

There was a baby with many chins. This is the story about how love helped me to grow.

This is me. I'm a baby. My name is Gabriel.

Oops! Sorry! This is me. I'm next to the blue bear.

I was born in a hospital in the winter. I got up pretty early to be born.

This is my mommy. She's a magical angel pixie fairy. I think she can fly.

She's always very elegant and lovely. So hard-working.

My mommy was very busy even before she met me.

Sometimes she had to put cream into her coffee while holding me ...

... And drink coffee while holding me.

Sometimes she ate over me, too, which was OK. I had crumbs in my hair.

Mommy always has to use her iPad. She does very important work all the time. She has to make music by touching dots. I think it helps people to call each other on the phone or something.

I love when mommy feeds me! I love formula and milk most of all. She feeds me all the time ...

... Mommy feeds me when we're out ...

... Mommy feeds me at the restaurant ...

... Mommy even feeds me with a spoon!

Mommy feeds me Korean dumpling!

Mommy even feeds me bagel!

Mommy even feeds me while getting fed herself! That's a mongey that I found in the kitchen. He helps out with things sometimes. I like to pick his nose.

Did I mention that I can eat mommy? She tastes so good!

Sleeping is a very important part about being a mommy ...

... Sometimes we sleep far apart ...

... Sometimes we sleep close together ...

... Sometimes we sleep on the couch ...

... And we even we sleep in the car!

No matter how we sleep or where we sleep ...

... Sleeping with my mommy is the best thing in the world!

Mommy celebrates my birthdays with me! I have a birthday almost every day.

This is my 30-day birthday. I don't remember it; I was sleeping.

This is my 100-day birthday. I gained some weight. Mommy says I'm fluffy.

And then we celebrated my 1-year birthday! We were surrounded by wild animals, but mommy protects me always.

I celebrated mommy's birthday, too! I bought a card for her and made a picture of me. The mongey said he did it instead, so we threw him in the backyard. My mommy turned 26 years old. That's what she tells me.

We have so much fun together! Mommy is my favorite playmate!

Mommy dressed me up as a bunny one time! That was fun. Ribbit, ribbit!

We walked like 5 miles for hunger! I got to throw apples onto the ground.

One time I met my brother, R2. He's a robot. He doesn't work very well. I think he's kind of slow.

Mommy took me to visit her school. She graduated from the best school in the world! Mommy's so smart. The mongey came along for protection. No one wants to fight a mongey! They throw poops.

We even met JFK. He didn't like to move, though.
